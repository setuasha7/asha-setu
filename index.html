About Us

We are a student-led initiative dedicated to spreading kindness and supporting underprivileged communities through meaningful action. Our project organizes a donation drive that collects gently used clothes from students, repairs them if needed, and distributes them to those in need before the winter season.

In addition to clothing, we raise cash donations to purchase essential items for a local orphanage—ensuring broader impact and care.

To make giving easier and more accessible, we’ve integrated Artificial Intelligence into our mission. Our website and chatbot help spread awareness, share important information, and guide donors through a simple, seamless donation process.

Together, we’re using compassion and technology to make a real difference.

Our core belief is that small, well-focused interventions — when executed with local collaboration — create lasting change. We aim to build bridges ("Setu") for hope ("Asha") across communities.

Our Mission

To inspire compassion and community involvement by supporting underprivileged individuals through clothing and essential item donations—while using technology to make giving more accessible, efficient, and impactful.
